Spanish Greetings

Learning a few greeting patterns makes beginner conversations much easier.

Common phrases

  • Hola means hello.
  • Buenos dias is used in the morning.
  • Buenas tardes is used later in the day.
  • Mucho gusto is a common way to say nice to meet you.

Study tip

Practice these aloud so you remember both the spelling and the rhythm.
